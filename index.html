I am Maxfield Green, a geospatial data scientist

With an excellent track record as a consultant in the utility industry, I bring a polished and innovative approach to modelling time series and geospatial processes. I graduated in 2019 from the University of Vermont with an MS in Data Science and Complex Systems. I'm fascinated by leveraging satellite imagery and remotely sensed time series data to better understand and model physical systems, ranging from wildfire spread to electrical outage forecasting.

I create tools that help folks make data driven decisions

By nature, I'm a curious and problem oriented person. I've worked as a technical consultant from idea conception all the way through model and product deployment. This means acting as a core data scientist, data story teller and project manager. My electric outage analytics tools have assisted utility leaders allocate annual vegetatation and asset management budgets, saving time, money and trees! Additionally, I have expertise in applying computer vision and deep learning to detect and segment landcover and objects in satellite imagery.
